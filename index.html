XAI · Clinical · Python

30-Day Hospital Readmission Risk

Solo Project · 2025

Built and explained a clinical risk model on 101,766 patient records, engineering 96 features from raw EHR data. Used Bayesian tuning (Optuna, 50 trials) and stratified 5-fold cross-validation to handle class imbalance. Achieved ROC AUC 0.67, consistent with published benchmarks for this task. The SHAP explainability dashboard was designed specifically for non-technical clinical users.

Scale

101,766 patient records, 96 engineered features, stratified 5-fold CV

Result

ROC AUC 0.67, in line with published clinical benchmarks for 30-day readmission prediction
